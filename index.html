About Broey.

A multi-talented musician and "real sound guy" who's been making music for over 15 years. With a background in multiple instruments and a passion for creating across genres, Broey.'s sound is diverse and constantly evolving. From chill lofi beats to EDM and everything in between, Broey.'s music defies genre boundaries and showcases his desire to push the limits of creativity.

Drawing inspiration from a wide range of artists, from Daft Punk and Skrillex to Tame Impala and Gorillaz, Broey.'s music is a unique blend of influences. In addition to music, Broey. is also inspired by photography, animation, visual arts, and film and TV, making his art truly multidimensional.

Under the aliases "Broey." and broeybeats, Broey. has released music on several platforms, with "Broey." representing his passion project, and broeybeats. serving as a place for him to make chill music with friends. Additionally, Broey. is very active on social media, regularly posting production tips and short live videos of his music. Currently, Broey. does not have any major upcoming releases, but promises new and exciting tunes in the short term.

You can follow Broey.'s journey and access his music, socials, and extras like podcast features and interviews through this link: https://linktr.ee/broeybeats

Broey. encourages fans to reach out and engage in discussions about art and music, as meeting new people has been the best part of his journey as an artist so far.
