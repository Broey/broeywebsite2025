## Visual Direction

The visual world should pull from late-night electronic music, blurred lights, audio hardware, glowing interfaces, cover art, studio gear, and underground internet culture.

The design should use:

* Deep charcoal / soft black / graphite backgrounds
* Warm off-white text

* Cards that feel tactile and dimensional
